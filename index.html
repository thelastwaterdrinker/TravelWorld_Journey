About Me

Can you believe it has been a whole year since I embarked on this incredible journey of exploration and discovery? Today, on my one-year travel anniversary, I find myself reminiscing about the warm and playful moments that Berlin, Bonito (Brazil), Citadelle (Belgium), Istanbul, and Fethiye have gifted me.

The aroma of Turkish delight lingered in the air, a sweet reminder of the delightful moments spent exploring this dynamic city.

As I reflect on these remarkable destinations, I can't help but feel grateful for the friendships formed, the cultures embraced, and the memories etched into the pages of my travel diary. Here's to another year of playful adventures and the countless stories yet to unfold!
